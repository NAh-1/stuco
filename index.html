Who We Are

Our Mission

Student Council 2026–2027 is the official representative body for all students. We bridge the gap between students and administration, champion your ideas, and bring the energy that makes school life memorable.

Whether it's organizing spirit weeks, advocating for better campus resources, fundraising for community causes, or celebrating student achievements — we show up for our school every single day.

Every decision, every event, every initiative starts with one question: what does our student body need?

01

Advocacy

Your voice reaches administration through us. We fight for the changes you want.

02

Events

From pep rallies to prom — we curate unforgettable moments all year long.

03

Community

We lift each other up through service, inclusivity, and shared purpose.

04

Excellence

We set the standard. High expectations, outstanding execution, every time.
